library library explores publication as a graphic design practice. Each issue orbits around a literary text and experiments with form and materiality through formats that challenge the dominant models of commercial graphic design and mainstream publishing. This project aims to build a community through collaborative making, and to connect with a small yet engaged publicthrough various forms of dissemination both analog and digital. library library considers the performative potential of publication, and asks questions about public institutions and shared spaces, libraries and archives, process, and design authorship.
